Our Mission
To provide affordable, accessible and high-quality healthcare to every individual regardless of their financial background. We believe no one should suffer due to lack of money or resources. MedEase is committed to bridging the gap between quality medical care and the common people of Vasai and surrounding areas.

Our Vision
To become the most trusted and technology-driven community hospital in Maharashtra — where every patient walks in with worry and walks out with hope. We aim to digitize healthcare delivery and make the OPD experience seamless, fast, and transparent for every patient and doctor.
Our Journey 📖
2011 — The Beginning
MedEase was founded in Vasai by Dr. Ramesh Nair with a small OPD clinic, 3 doctors, and a vision to provide affordable healthcare to the local community.
2013 — Expansion
Opened 3 new departments — Pediatrics, Dermatology, and Orthopedics. Patient footfall crossed 5,000 annually.
2016 — Community Outreach
Launched free health camps in rural areas around Vasai. Treated over 2,000 underprivileged patients at zero cost.
2019 — Digital Push
Introduced digital records and online appointment booking for the first time. Reduced average patient wait time by 40%.
2022 — New Building
Inaugurated a new 3-floor hospital building with 50 beds, modern OT, and a dedicated emergency ward.
2024 — MedEase Digital System
Launched this fully digital OPD Management System with AI chatbot, UPI payments, and live queue tracking — making MedEase one of the most tech-forward community hospitals in the region.
